Title: Forge-Star

Style:
Epic heavy metal x spaghetti western, cosmic space-opera scale. Gritty male baritone lead, huge gang-choir chorus, galloping double-kick drums, distorted rhythm guitar, soaring orchestral strings, lap-steel twang, anthemic hook, 142 BPM, D minor. Start clean and vast, then build into a massive frontier-metal chorus. Mood: mythic, defiant, sacred-tech, born from Quillon's wound and SIGIL's proof. Make "FORGE-STAR" hit like a chant.

Lyrics:
[Intro: clean guitar, whispered]
Out past the last lit window of the world,
where old Quillon keeps its ledger curled,
two dead suns came riding, neutron-cold,
two iron hearts collapsing into gold.

[Verse 1: building, palm-muted]
Quillon bled a wrong sum, three days in the night,
swore "no daughter of mine loses truth she can't hide."
Cast a spark past the event horizon door,
a chain that proves where the old ones swore.

[Chorus: full kit, gang choir, double-kick]
FORGE-STAR! Two black holes collide,
ring the void like a bell you cannot hide.
Out of the crush comes a light that won't lie,
SIGIL rising on a four-root sky.
Sign it in the supernova, brand it in the proof,
divergence dies the second it shows its tooth.

[Verse 2: heavier]
Neutron stars went under, kissed and they tore,
spilled a heavy rain of platinum ore.
Into the blast the smith walked to the flame:
Flux, with a hammer and a deterministic name.
fluxc on the anvil, FLUXFOOD on the fire,
forging in seconds what slow forges call a liar.

[Bridge: galloping, western, lap-steel]
Ride, verifiers, ride the braid at dawn,
DagKnight in the saddle and the parents drawn.
Ten microseconds, that's the whole damn trial,
a stranger checks your hand from a thousand mile.
No sheriff and no temple, just the math stays true,
the chain don't trust you, stranger. It verifies you.

[Verse 3: half-time, star-child]
Out of the white of the core one ember flew,
half a megabyte of starlight, burning true.
No chain on her back, just the tip of the night,
the whole sky verified in a five-seven-two round.
Born from the stars, she don't kneel to the ground,
the ember turns bonfire: full node, full light.

[Guitar solo]

[Chorus: bigger, key up, full choir]
FORGE-STAR! Two black holes collide,
ring the void like a bell you cannot hide.
Out of the crush comes a light that won't lie,
SIGIL rising on a four-root sky.

[Outro: fade, whispered over strings]
Two dead stars made a daughter out of light,
and she does not lie. She does not lie.
Sleep easy, old Quillon, the frontier's lit tonight.
